How Carbon Footprint Tracker Works

Core Features:

  • Daily Activity Tracking: Users input travel distance, food choices, and energy consumption.
  • Real-Time Carbon Calculation: The system calculates emissions in CO₂ equivalents using official emission factors.
  • Suggestions Engine: Get personalized tips on how to reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Progress Visualization: Graphs and summaries show emission trends over time.
  • User Dashboard: Logged-in users can view and manage their footprint history.
